Explain what a transformer model is in simple terms.
A

A transformer is a type of neural network architecture that's especially good at understanding sequences of data — like text, code, or even DNA.

The key innovation is the attention mechanism. Instead of reading text word-by-word like older models, a transformer can look at all words at once and figure out which ones are most relevant to each other.

Think of it like reading a sentence: you don't just understand each word in isolation — you understand how "it" refers to "the cat" three words back. That's what attention does computationally.

Wikipedia: Transformer (deep learning) Attention Is All You Need (2017)
